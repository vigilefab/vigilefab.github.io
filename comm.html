Website

My experience with website design and development dates all the way back to the 1990s. In my pre-teens, I had a fascination for HTML-Coding. For the longest time in the late 90s, I managed and operated a huge fansite for a Holywood actor I admired (I bet you don't know him). After that, I moved to blogging and designing layouts for blogs. I also had a good number of website development or design commissions. Though all that dwindled when I started doing my PhD, I recently rekindled my web-management skill with the Halo-Halo Network.

The Halo-Halo Network

I was the first webmaster of the Halo-Halo Network website when it started in 2021. I managed the site singlehandedly for four years, where I wrote blog posts and edited blog contributions by members of the community.

Central Visayan Institute Foundation (CVIF)
